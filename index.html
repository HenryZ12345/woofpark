WOOF PARK

So you havent been home all day? But your dog HAS been home all day. Its time to take him on his routinely walk. But dont you get tired of following routines. Take him somewhere fun. Take him to WOOF PARK. A community of dogs and owners who share locations in order for the Woofs to interact,play, and distress.

WHERE?

WOOF-PARK operates like a social media platforms with key meetup locations at or around dogparks, fields, and large parks. Our meet-up locations are established around all of the NYC's 5 Boros but you are not restricted to these locations. WOOF-PARK encourages owners to network and build meaningful connections through and FOR their dogs. Free to attened

How to Join

Download the WOOF App for IOS or Android. Create a profile and start walking. You can also sign up for our newsletters for upcoming events and trips. WOOF's community is based on trust so feel free to play with toys left at locations. Toy donations welcomed!
